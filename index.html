FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions
Who is EoH?

EoH is a technology company, specializing in IoT platform development to support and accompany developers and smart solutions providers for many different vertical industries. Find out more.

What is E-Ra IoT Platform?

An application that allows software developers and IoT devices to track development results on a beautiful, consistent and professional interface. In addition, with a set of libraries developed by EoH, it will make it easier for developers to connect IoT devices and manage them as easily as possible!

Who is E-Ra IoT Platform for?

For students, IoT developers to implement and manage various IoT projects on their own.

I'm an IoT technology enthusiast but I have no hardware programming experience, can I use E-Ra?

E-Ra application is a dedicated application for IoT programmers, IT students who are passionate about IoT to be able to program and build their own independent IoT project. Therefore, you need to learn hardware programming if you want to use E-Ra IoT Platform.

Who will support me in the process of using E-Ra?

During experience and use of E-Ra, if you have any questions or have problems that need help, the EoH team will always be here to support you.

FAQs

Can I try E-Ra IoT Platform for free?

Of course, E-Ra IoT Platform will always have a 6 months Free Trial plan with limited features for users.

Which payment method is accepted?

Currently, during Soft Launch, E-Ra IoT Platform only accepts payment via bank transfer according to the transfer information provided when contacting E-Ra Sales Team.

Can I change my Subscription Plan?

Yes, you can update, change or cancel your plan at any time. If you need help changing your Subscription Plan, please contact us at our support number: (+84) 906998291

Does E-Ra offer a refund for the remaining time of registered Plan that have not been used?

Once you have signed up for any Subscription Plan, there is no refund option. You can cancel your existing Subscription Plan to prevent it from auto-renew.

Do I have full access with my registered Subscription Plan?

Yes. You will have full access to all features listed under the detailed features comparison for each Subscription Plan.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription whenever you want with no commitment at the Payment section.

When I switch from my current Plan to another, will I get a refund for the remaining time of the current Plan? From when is the validity period of the new Plan?

Once you complete the Payment for your new Plan, the validity period of the new Plan will start from this point forward. E-Ra IoT Platform will not issue a refund in the event that you still have the remaining time of the old Plan.
